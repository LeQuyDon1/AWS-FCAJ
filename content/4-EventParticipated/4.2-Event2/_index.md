# Reflection Report – "Meetup 30/05"

### Purpose of the Event

- Share practical knowledge and experiences in Cloud Computing, AI, and software development.
- Introduce AWS learning resources and career development opportunities.
- Improve both technical and soft skills through real-world sharing sessions.
- Connect members of the First Cloud AI Journey community and encourage continuous learning.

- **Why We Always Need Confidence**
  - Nguyen Thi Quynh Nhu
- **The Hidden Iceberg of a Project: DevOps Before Disaster**

- **Hackathon – "HA! a tons of fun"**

  - Guest Speaker

### Highlights of the Event

#### Why We Always Need Confidence

- Confidence is an essential skill for both academic and professional success.
- Students should actively ask questions, present ideas, and embrace challenges.
- Confidence is built through continuous learning and practical experience.

#### The Hidden Iceberg of a Project: DevOps Before Disaster

- Many project failures originate from hidden issues rather than visible technical problems.
- DevOps practices improve collaboration between development and operations teams.
- CI/CD, automation, monitoring, and infrastructure management help reduce project risks.

#### Hackathon Experience

- The speakers shared valuable experiences from participating in hackathons.
- Effective teamwork and communication are critical under time constraints.
- Building a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) quickly is an important practical skill.
- Hackathons provide excellent opportunities to improve problem-solving abilities.

#### Level Up Your AWS Skills with Cloud Quest and Floci

- Introduction to AWS Cloud Quest as an interactive learning platform.
- Learning AWS through gamification makes cloud concepts easier to understand.
- Demonstrated how Cloud Quest and Floci help learners gain hands-on AWS experience.
- Shared an effective roadmap for beginners pursuing AWS certifications.

#### The Iceberg of Procrastination

- Explained that procrastination is often caused by fear, uncertainty, or perfectionism rather than laziness.
- Introduced practical techniques for time management and productivity.
- Encouraged participants to develop consistent study and working habits.

### What I Learned

#### Soft Skills

- The importance of confidence in communication and presentations.
- Better teamwork and collaboration techniques.
- Time management strategies to overcome procrastination.

#### Technical Knowledge

- A deeper understanding of DevOps principles and modern software development workflows.
- The value of automation, CI/CD pipelines, and continuous monitoring.
- Effective ways to learn AWS using Cloud Quest.
- Practical insights into hackathons and real-world software development.

#### Career Development

- Continuous learning is essential in the rapidly evolving cloud industry.
- Participating in technical communities provides valuable networking opportunities.
- Building both technical expertise and soft skills is equally important for career growth.

### Applying the Knowledge

- Practice DevOps concepts in future software projects.
- Participate in hackathons to improve practical development skills.
- Continue learning AWS services through AWS Cloud Quest and hands-on labs.
- Improve presentation and communication skills by sharing knowledge with peers.
- Develop better study plans to minimize procrastination and increase productivity.

### My Experience at the Event

Attending the **First Cloud AI Journey Sharing Day** was an inspiring and valuable experience. The event combined technical knowledge with personal development, making it beneficial for students interested in Cloud Computing and Artificial Intelligence.

#### Learning from Experienced Speakers

The speakers shared practical experiences instead of only theoretical concepts. Their stories helped me understand how technical skills, teamwork, and confidence contribute to success in both academic projects and professional careers.

#### Practical Technical Knowledge

The DevOps session provided a clearer understanding of software development practices, especially the importance of automation, CI/CD, and monitoring. The Cloud Quest presentation introduced an engaging way to learn AWS through interactive challenges, while the Hackathon sharing session demonstrated how real-world projects require effective collaboration and rapid problem-solving.

#### Personal Development

One of the most memorable sessions was **Why We Always Need Confidence**, which emphasized that confidence is a skill developed through practice rather than something people are naturally born with. The final presentation on procrastination also encouraged me to improve my productivity by understanding the deeper causes behind delaying important tasks.

#### Networking Opportunities

The event also provided opportunities to interact with other students and AWS community members. Listening to different experiences broadened my perspective on learning cloud technologies and planning my future career.

### Lessons Learned

- Confidence is developed through continuous practice and experience.
- DevOps is not only about tools but also about collaboration and efficient workflows.
- Hackathons are excellent environments for improving technical and teamwork skills.
- AWS Cloud Quest is an effective platform for gaining practical AWS experience.
- Success in technology requires both technical knowledge and strong soft skills.
