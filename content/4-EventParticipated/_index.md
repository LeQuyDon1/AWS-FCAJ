Throughout my journey with the **First Cloud AI Journey (FCAJ)** community, I actively participated in workshops, meetups, and knowledge-sharing events organized by the community. These events provided valuable opportunities to stay up to date with the latest trends in **Cloud Computing, DevOps, and Generative AI**, while learning from AWS experts and experienced engineers working in the technology industry.

By attending these events, I not only strengthened my technical knowledge but also improved essential soft skills such as communication, teamwork, problem-solving, and career planning. In addition, I had the opportunity to connect with other community members, expand my professional network, and gain motivation to continue learning AWS services and applying AI technologies in real-world projects.

During my internship, I participated in **two major events**, which are described below.

---

### [Event 1](4.1-Event1/)

**Event Name:** FCAJ Community Day (First Cloud Journey Community)

**Date:** May 2026

**Location:** AWS Vietnam Office, Bitexco Financial Tower, Ho Chi Minh City

**Role:** Participant

**Overview:**

This event focused on topics related to **Generative AI, Cloud Computing, AWS Architecture, DevOps, and career development**. Industry experts shared practical experiences in building cloud solutions on AWS, discussed the latest AI trends, introduced best practices for system architecture, and provided valuable insights into hackathons and professional growth in the technology industry.

---

### [Event 2](4.2-Event2/)

**Event Name:** Meetup 30/5 – First Cloud AI Journey Sharing Day

**Date:** May 30, 2026

**Location:** AWS Vietnam Office, Bitexco Financial Tower, Ho Chi Minh City

**Role:** Participant

**Overview:**

This meetup featured several inspiring presentations, including **Why We Always Need Confidence**, **The Hidden Iceberg of a Project: DevOps Before Disaster**, **Hackathon Experience**, **Level Up Your AWS Skills with Cloud Quest and Floci**, and **The Iceberg of Procrastination**. The sessions helped me gain a deeper understanding of DevOps practices, AWS learning resources, teamwork, personal development, and effective time management.

---

Through these two events, I gained valuable practical knowledge of AWS, DevOps, and Artificial Intelligence while improving important soft skills required in the workplace. These experiences also helped me develop a clearer career direction toward becoming a Cloud Engineer and motivated me to continue learning, building real-world projects, and actively contributing to the AWS community in the future.
